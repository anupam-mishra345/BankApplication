Diversity

Employees at Ocean Bank 2.0 enjoy a nurturing and dynamic work environment. We are proud to be a India-based institution where diversity plays an important role in the region's continued growth and development. As such, not only do we embrace our community's diversity, but we also encourage workplace diversity. We value the many differences in our employees, customers and vendors. Diversity is the key to our continued success in our marketplace.





Employment Practices

As an Equal Opportunity and Affirmative Action employer, we are committed to diversity through our recruiting, hiring and retention practices. It is the policy of Ocean Bank to offer employment opportunities to all qualified employees and applicants, regardless of race, sex, color, religion, national origin, age, sexual orientation, or disability.

Ocean Bank 2.0 will provide reasonable accommodation to all applicants and employees.
