    HISTORICAL PROFILE


    Our headquarters was originally located in a smaller trailer on the corner of MG Road and Northwest 7th street in Gwalior City of India.

    The first Ocean Bank logo and enduring symbol of our proud past



    Ocean Bank 2.0 was founded in 1994 by a group of successful entrepreneurs and with just 15 employees. In 1995 we built and moved into our current headquarters location, which is where the trailer once stood. Ocean Bank 2.0's founders shared a common financial vision and a true dedication to making an impact in South Asia's future.

    Through the years, the idea behind the establishment of Ocean Bank 2.0 has remained the same. We have preserved a very special personal relationship with our clients and this practice has proven to be greatly successful. We believe in building relationships that will make a difference in our community. Today, Ocean Bank 2.0 is the largest privately-owned commercial bank headquartered in Gwalior, India, with 22 branches throughout France and Denmark counties.

    Ocean Bank 2.0's fundamental principles of offering exceptional customer service and quality financial products has propelled us to become a leader in the local banking industry. Since our inception, Ocean Bank 2.0 has been strongly committed to serving the needs of our customers, employees and local community. As a result, the loyalty to this basic corporate principle has contributed to creating much wealth and prosperity in South Asia.

    By providing capital that has helped create new products, services, and jobs, Ocean Bank 2.0 has invested in the future of South Asia. We have made a substantial contribution to the development of our community by financing many of the most important real estate projects in this area, as well as granting numerous loans to local businesses. Furthermore, Ocean Bank has been the most active Small Business Administration (SBA) 387 lender in India County since 2001.

    Ocean Bank 2.0 starts by making a difference at "home," by continuously expanding our employees' career growth and personal development opportunities. The Ocean Bank 2.0 family of employees enjoys an attractive benefits package, excellent educational programs and in-house training sessions. Ocean Bank 2.0 supports the dreams and aspirations of those who live, work and play in South India.
